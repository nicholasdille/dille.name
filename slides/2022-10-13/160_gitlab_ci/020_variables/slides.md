## Variables

---

## Flavors

Variables can be...

- defined in the pipeline definition
- predefined by GitLab
- defined in the UI

### Hands-On

1. Update files from `src/`
1. `variables` in definition:

    ```yaml
    job_name:
      variables:
        version: dev
      script:
      #...
      - go build -o hello -ldflags "-X main.Version=${version}" .
    ```
